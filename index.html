OUR STORY

Founded in 2018 by brothers Malik and Jamal King, KingBro Empire began as a small streetwear line in Brooklyn and has grown into a globally recognized fashion brand.

Our mission is to blend urban aesthetics with premium quality, creating clothing that makes you feel regal while staying true to street culture roots.

Every piece is designed with intention, crafted with care, and made to help you command attention wherever you go.
